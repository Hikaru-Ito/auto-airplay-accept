The moment an AirPlay dialog appears, the Accept button is clicked. You don't have to do anything.

Multilingual

Detects "Accept", "Allow", "受け入れる", and more. Works regardless of your macOS display language.

Fully background

No Dock icon, no menu bar item. Negligible CPU and memory footprint. Stays out of your way.

Open source
